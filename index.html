RETIRE IN HELL

  • Sins of the Wolf

    • Layer 1

    The first layer of Hell known as Limbo and hold the virtuous pagans. The only way to get to this layer is to get ferried across by Charon across the Acheron. The punishment is being away from God.

    Layer 2

    The second layer of Hell which hold the lustful. Here is where Minos resigns, who gives out judgement to all who go through hell. There punishment is being blown around by winds for all eternity.

    Layer 3

    The third layer of Hell which is the layer of the Gluttonous. On this layer, there is a perpetual rain and the residents here are being mauled by Cerberus for all eternity.

    Layer 4

    The fouth layer of Hell which hold the Hoarders and Spendthrifts. The punishment here is that they have to roll huge rocks against eachother for all eternity.

    Layer 5

    Layer 5 or the layer of the Wrathful is located at the Marsh of Styx. There the people who are punsished have to stay in the boiling river.

    Layer 6

    Layer 6 is the Heretics located inside the City of Dis. These people are stuck inside of burning tombs watched over by demons.

    Layer 7

    The next layer is the people who commited violent sins. There are tyrants who are in a boiling river, people who commited suicide and have become trees who are constantly dying, and people who commited violence against God, Nature and Art who are on the burning sands.

    Layer 8

    The eighth layer of Hell, which contains all ten Malbowges.

    Bowge 1

    The first Bowge holds the Panders and Seducers. These people are being scourged by demons.

    Bowge 2

    The second Bowge contains the Flatterers who are stuck in a pit of filth.

    Bowge 3

    The third Bowge hold the Simoniacs who are in a pit stacked on top of eachother with the top persons feet getting burned.

    Bowge 4

    The fourth Bowge has the Sorcerers whose punishment is that have a twisted head and are forced to walk backward.

    Bowge 5

    The fifth Bowge are the Barrators who are stuck in a boiling pitch with demons guarding them.

    Bowge 6

    The sixth Bowge has the Hypocrites. There punishment is that they must walk in circles with a coat lined lead.

    Bowge 7

    The seventh Bowge contains the Thieves. The thieves have many punishments which involve changing their appearences.

    Bowge 8

    The eight Bowge holds the Counsellors of Fraud. Their souls are wrapped up into flames.

    Bowge 9

    The ninth Bowge are the Sowers of Discord, whose punishment is being smitten by a Demon with a sword.

    Bowge 10

    The tenth Bowge has the Falsifiers, whose punishment is having horrible sickness.

    Layer 9

    The last layer of Hell and has people who commited the sins of treachery. These people are divided into Caina, Antenora, Ptolomaea and Judecca. Their punishments differ, but all are stuck inside of the frozen lake Cocytus.
